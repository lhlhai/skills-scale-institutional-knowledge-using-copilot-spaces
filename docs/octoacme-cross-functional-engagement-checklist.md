## Purpose
Ensure that all relevant cross-functional roles are identified and engaged at the appropriate project phases to improve outcomes, reduce rework, and maintain accountability.

## When to use
- During **Project Initiation**: to identify which cross-functional roles are needed
- During **Project Planning**: to confirm engagement and define collaboration touchpoints
- During **Execution**: to ensure ongoing involvement as needed

## How to use
Review the checklist below and mark applicable roles. For each role engaged, document the contact person, expected involvement level, and key touchpoints.

## Additional Considerations

### Communication Plan
- [ ] Cross-functional roles added to project communication channels (Slack, email lists)
- [ ] Meeting cadence defined for cross-functional reviews
- [ ] Escalation path established for cross-functional dependencies

### Documentation
- [ ] Cross-functional requirements captured in project docs
- [ ] Role-specific deliverables and timelines documented
- [ ] Integration points and dependencies tracked in project plan

---

## Tips for Effective Cross-functional Collaboration
- **Engage early**: Involve cross-functional roles during initiation and planning, not just execution
- **Define clear expectations**: Document involvement level, deliverables, and timelines upfront
- **Maintain regular touchpoints**: Schedule recurring reviews or checkpoints, not just ad-hoc requests
- **Close the loop**: Share outcomes and learnings with cross-functional partners during retrospectives
